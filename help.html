Answer

🎮 How to Play:

Choose a game from the Games tab, select your entry amount, upload a photo of your card, and submit. Your entry will be reviewed by admin.

💡 Tip: Make sure your card photo is clear for faster approval.
⏳ Round Time:

The current round countdown is shown on the home page. Each round lasts 5 days. Once the timer hits zero, winners are drawn.

📅 New rounds start every 5 days at 10:00 AM.
💬 Contact Support:

You can reach our support team instantly via the chat bubble on the home screen or by clicking the "Live Chat" button above.

⏱️ Support is available 24/7. Average response time: 1-2 minutes.
💰 How to Deposit:

Go to Wallet → Deposit or tap Load & Play on the home screen. Select your card type, enter amount, and upload a photo.

💳 Supported cards: Apple Card, Razer Gold, Steam Card.
🏆 How to Win:

Predict 12 matches correctly to win the jackpot! You can also win with 10 or 11 correct predictions.

🎯 Prize structure: 12/12 = Jackpot, 11/12 = 2nd Prize, 10/12 = 3rd Prize.
