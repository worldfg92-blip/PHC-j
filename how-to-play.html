How to Play Jackpot

The PHC Jackpot is a 12-match prediction game where you predict the outcome of 12 selected football matches. Your goal is to correctly predict as many match results as possible to win amazing cash prizes!

🎯 The Goal: Predict the outcome of all 12 matches correctly (Home Win, Draw, or Away Win) to win the JACKPOT PRIZE!

📊 How It Works

Each round features 12 real football matches from top leagues around the world. For each match, you must choose one of three possible outcomes:

Home Win 1 Home team wins
Draw X Match ends in draw
Away Win 2 Away team wins

🏅 Prize Structure

The jackpot prize pool is distributed based on how many predictions you get correct:

🏆 12/12 Jackpot Win the Grand Prize!
🥈 11/12 2nd Prize 11 correct predictions
🥉 10/12 3rd Prize 10 correct predictions

💡 Tip: The more matches you predict correctly, the bigger your prize! Even if you don't get all 12, you can still win with 10 or 11 correct predictions.

🎮

How to Take Part

Follow these simple steps to enter the PHC Jackpot and start winning:

  • 1 Login or Create an Account
    Sign in to your PHC Jackpot account. If you're new, create a free account in seconds.
  • 2 Select Your Card Type
    Choose from Apple Card, Razer Gold, or Steam Card. Each card has different entry fees and prize levels.
  • 3 Choose Your Entry Level
    Select your entry amount ($500, $1,500, $2,000, or $3,500). Higher entries offer bigger jackpot prizes!
  • 4 Upload Card Photo
    Take a clear photo of your chosen card and upload it for verification. This is required to validate your entry.
  • 5 Predict 12 Matches
    Go to the Predictor tab and select your prediction (1, X, or 2) for each of the 12 matches.
  • 6 Submit & Play
    Click "Play to Win" to submit your predictions. Your entry will be validated and you'll be in the running for the jackpot!
  • 7 Wait for Results
    After the round ends (5-day countdown), winners are announced. Check the Previous Results tab to see if you won!

⏰ Important: Each round lasts 5 days. Make sure to submit your predictions before the countdown ends to be eligible for the jackpot!

💡

Quick Tips & Rules

  • ✓ Research the Matches
    Check team form, injuries, and head-to-head records before making your predictions.
  • ✓ One Entry Per Round
    You can only submit one prediction per round. Make it count!
  • ✓ Valid Card Required
    Your uploaded card photo must be clear and valid. Fake or invalid entries will be rejected.
  • ✓ Winners Announced
    Winners are announced immediately after each round ends. Check the "Previous Results" tab.
  • ✓ Prizes Credited
    All winnings are credited directly to your PHC wallet. You can withdraw anytime!

🔒 Fair Play: All entries are verified by our admin team. Cheating or fraudulent entries will result in immediate disqualification.
